Seven proven learning techniques:

2. **Spaced Repetition** - Review material at increasing intervals for long-term memory
3. **Active Recall** - Test yourself frequently instead of passive re-reading
4. **Problem-Solving Practice** - Work through many examples to build intuition
5. **Group Study** - Collaborate to identify weak areas and learn from peers
6. **Visual Learning** - Draw diagrams and mind maps for complex concepts
7. **Consistent Schedule** - Regular study sessions beat cramming for retention
