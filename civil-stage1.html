Foundation Study Roadmap

Follow this weekly plan while completing your daily tasks

Week 1 — Math for Engineers

  • Linear equations & algebra basics
  • Functions and graphs
  • Basic trigonometry (sin, cos, tan)
  • Units & conversions

Goal: Be comfortable manipulating equations and using formulas

Week 2 — Physics Fundamentals

  • Force and motion (Newton's Laws)
  • Mass vs Weight
  • Basic vectors
  • Gravity and acceleration

Goal: Understand how forces affect objects

Week 3 — Engineering Basics

  • What is Civil Engineering?
  • Types of structures (bridges, buildings, roads)
  • Loads (dead load vs live load)
  • Safety factors

Goal: Learn how engineers think about structures

Week 4 — Problem Solving & Tools

  • Rearranging formulas
  • Solving word problems
  • Using a calculator correctly
  • Basic graph reading

Goal: Be able to solve simple engineering-style problems
