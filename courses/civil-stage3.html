Specialization Study Roadmap

Develop expertise in your chosen civil engineering discipline

Week 1 — Structural Engineering Basics

  • Analysis of complex structures
  • Reinforced concrete design
  • Steel structure design
  • Lateral load design (wind, earthquakes)

Goal: Design safe and efficient structures

Week 2 — Geotechnical Engineering

  • Soil mechanics and classification
  • Foundation design
  • Slope stability analysis
  • Bearing capacity calculations

Goal: Understand soil behavior and foundation design

Week 3 — Transportation Engineering

  • Road design and standards
  • Pavement materials and design
  • Traffic flow analysis
  • Bridge design for traffic

Goal: Design transportation infrastructure

Week 4 — Environmental & Water Resources

  • Water treatment systems
  • Stormwater management
  • Wastewater treatment design
  • Sustainable infrastructure

Goal: Design environmentally responsible projects
