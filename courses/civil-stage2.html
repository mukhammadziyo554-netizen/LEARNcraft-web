Core Concepts Study Roadmap

Build your understanding of structural mechanics and materials

Week 1 — Statics Fundamentals

  • Equilibrium equations (ΣF = 0, ΣM = 0)
  • Free body diagrams
  • Support reactions
  • Internal forces and moments

Goal: Analyze forces in static structures

Week 2 — Beam Analysis

  • Beam types and loading conditions
  • Shear force and bending moment diagrams
  • Cantilever and simply supported beams
  • Distributed loads

Goal: Understand how beams behave under load

Week 3 — Material Properties

  • Stress and strain relationships
  • Elastic modulus (Young's modulus)
  • Ductility vs brittleness
  • Common materials: steel, concrete, timber

Goal: Learn how materials respond to forces

Week 4 — Structural Design Basics

  • Design codes and standards
  • Factor of safety
  • Load combinations
  • Simple column design

Goal: Understand design principles used in practice
