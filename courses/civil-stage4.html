Practical Application Study Roadmap

Build a portfolio of real-world engineering solutions

Week 1 — Case Study Analysis

  • Analyze iconic structures (Sydney Opera House, Burj Khalifa)
  • Understand design challenges and solutions
  • Learn from engineering failures and successes
  • Document lessons learned

Goal: Learn from real-world examples

Week 2 — Design Project 1

  • Design a simple bridge structure
  • Create technical drawings and calculations
  • Justify your design choices
  • Estimate project costs

Goal: Complete your first design project

Week 3 — Design Project 2

  • Design building foundations for different soil conditions
  • Perform stability analysis
  • Create construction drawings
  • Consider environmental impact

Goal: Apply geotechnical knowledge

Week 4 — Capstone Project Planning

  • Choose your capstone project topic
  • Research existing solutions
  • Outline project scope and timeline
  • Identify resources needed

Goal: Plan your capstone project
